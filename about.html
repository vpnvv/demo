Architecture is not about SPACE but about TIME

Ar.Mahima Sarada is a staunch lover of art, history and loves travelling. This has a direct influence on her style of design and vision.

Ar.Mahima Sarada has completed her B.ARCH from RVS School of Architecture, Coimbatore and M.ARCH from SVS College of Architecture , Coimbatore. Started her Career as Educationalist and Junior Architect, Mahima presently working in Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) , Palakkad.

She believes that collaboration starts at Design level. Design is a collaborative process, a dialogue, between client and architect. The passion for traditional Architecture comes from converting these spaces into comprehensive design solutions, which blend into the cultural, physical and emotional needs of the occupants. Mahima is not attached to a singular style of design and thus do not lose her creativity and interest to explore newer and bolder ideas. She is driven by a passion for excellence driving them to improve and innovate in all areas and every stage of design development while staying abreast with the latest in technology to ensure consistent high quality work. Mahimas emphasis is on making design holistic, contextual and environment friendly.
